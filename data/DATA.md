# Data

This repository does not redistribute the raw hyperspectral, Zarr, derived NumPy, or trained model files used during local analysis.

The analysis uses data associated with:

Jarocinska, A., Kopec, D., Niedzielko, J. et al. The utility of airborne hyperspectral and satellite multispectral images in identifying Natura 2000 non-forest habitats for conservation purposes. Scientific Reports 13, 4549 (2023). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-023-31705-6

The local project used airborne hyperspectral and RGB data provided directly by the study authors for research/proposal development. Raw data access should be requested from the corresponding author of the original publication.


Small hand-labeled PNG masks are included in `labels/masks` as project annotations:

- `water_mask.png`
- `soil_mask.png`
- `canopy_mask.png`
- `grass_mask.png`
